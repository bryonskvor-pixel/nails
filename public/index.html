Got Questions?

Everything You
Need to Know

How do I book an appointment? +
Just DM @nails.by.marissa.211 on Instagram! Tell me what you're thinking — inspo pics are always welcome — and we'll lock in a time that works for you.
Where are you located? +
I work out of my home in LaGrange, Ohio year-round, and I'm available on the Denison University campus during the school year. Location details are shared when you book.
How long does a full set take? +
Most sets take between 1.5 to 2.5 hours depending on length, design complexity, and add-ons. I'll give you a better estimate when you share what you're going for!
Do you take inspo photos? +
Absolutely — yes please! Send me everything you love. The more I know about your vision, the better I can bring it to life. Pinterest boards, Instagram saves, all of it.
Can you do custom designs? +
Custom is kind of my whole thing. Whether you want florals, ghosts, minimalist lines, or something totally wild — I love a challenge. Just ask.
What forms of payment do you accept? +
Venmo and cash are both great. We can sort out the details when you book!
